Frequently asked questions from customer to frontend developer

What technologies do you use?

Customers may be interested in what frameworks, libraries, and programming languages you use to build web applications.

What experience do you have in creating mobile-adaptive and responsive interfaces?

Here, the customer may ask about your experience in creating interfaces that look good and work on different devices and screen resolutions.

How do you ensure security and data protection?

This is an important question for customers who want to make sure their data will be secure from leaks or unauthorized access.

What is your testing and quality assurance process?

Customers may be interested in your testing methods, including unit testing, integration testing, and test automation.

What are your web application performance optimization capabilities?

This question may be important to customers who want their web applications to be fast and resource efficient.
